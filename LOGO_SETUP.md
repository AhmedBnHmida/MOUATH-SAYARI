# 🏛️ Logo Setup Instructions

## How to Add Your Logo

The website is now fully styled with a **professional Yellow and Black theme**, and your logo is ready to be displayed!

### Steps to Add the Logo:

1. **Save the logo image** you provided as `logo.png`
2. **Place it in the `images/` folder** at this path:
   ```
   c:\medenine\medenine-website\images\logo.png
   ```

### Logo Specifications:

- **Filename**: `logo.png`
- **Recommended Size**: 60x60 pixels (square format works best)
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Position**: Will appear in the header on the left side
- **Styling**: Automatically styled with yellow glow effect

### Logo Display:

Once added, your logo will:
- Appear in the top-left corner of the header
- Be displayed on all pages (header is shared across the site)
- Have a professional yellow drop-shadow effect
- Be responsive on mobile devices
- Have hover effects for better interactivity
